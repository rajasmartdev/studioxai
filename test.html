Study Tours

Observation-Driven Field Education

Study tours are designed for deeper engagement with specific ecological and ethical themes. Available as additions to camp programs or as standalone educational experiences.

Broken Forest Study Tour

Understanding deforestation, biodiversity loss, and forest regeneration through direct observation. The tour addresses three fundamental questions:

  1. What is a forest?
  2. What is the state of India's forests?
  3. How many saplings does it take to replace a single tree?

Bamboo Ecosystems Tour

Sustainable applications, growth patterns, and ecological significance of bamboo within forest systems.

Ecology & Ethics Program

Environmental responsibility, carrying capacity, and the relationship between human activity and ecosystem health.
